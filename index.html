Matchmaker

Are you searching for true love, a boyfriend, or even just someone to get coffee with? Well then, you're in luck! Look no further, Matchmaker could be the solution.

This simple website will ask you five questions and gauge our compatibility using your responses. Pretty neat, right? The questions each have the same five answer choices, ranging from 'strongly agree' to 'strongly disagree'.

After the 'Submit' button is clicked your results will instantly be displayed. If you'd like to see our compatibility for each question rather than the total, simply open the console in your browser (shortcut is typically F12 key). Anyways, enough introduction, you can begin at any time by clicking the 'Start' button!
